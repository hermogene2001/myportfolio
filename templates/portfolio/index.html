About Me

Hello! I'm Hermogene

UKUNDAYEZU Hermogene is an IT and software development professional with strong experience in full-stack web development, embedded systems, and smart technology solutions. He has worked extensively with PHP, Django, JavaScript, Bootstrap, MySQL, and modern web hosting environments to build investment platforms, e-commerce systems, role-based dashboards, and real-time data applications. Hermogene has led and developed multiple projects involving client–admin–agent systems, financial and investment applications with dynamic profit cycles, GPS-based tracking systems, and payment-controlled platforms. He also has hands-on experience with IoT and microcontrollers such as ESP32, ESP8266 (NodeMCU), Raspberry Pi, and various sensors, integrating hardware with software dashboards for monitoring and automation. In addition, he is actively exploring AI-powered solutions, including voice translation systems like Smart Umusemuzi, designed to bridge language barriers between English and Kinyarwanda using embedded systems and AI technologies. His work reflects a strong focus on problem-solving, scalability, and social impact, particularly in finance, smart infrastructure, and inclusive technology. Hermogene is passionate about building practical, real-world systems that combine software engineering, hardware integration, and user-centered design to solve local and global challenges.
